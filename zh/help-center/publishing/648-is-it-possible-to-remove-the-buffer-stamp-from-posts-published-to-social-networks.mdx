# Is it possible to remove the "Aitoearn" stamp from 帖子 published to social networks? Some 帖子 shared through Aitoearn 将会 display a source stamp or hashtag 那个 mentions 那个 您 used Aitoearn to 分享 the 帖子. 这个 source stamp is something 那个 is added by the social network and some customers have asked if it's possible to remove the mention of Aitoearn. Sadly, 这个 is not possible as it's something 那个 这些 social networks require of all third party apps. 这个 article outlines 哪里 您 将会 see 这些 stamps.
