# 入门指南 with Aitoearn's 发布功能 功能 The first step to 入门指南 with Aitoearn is to 连接 您的 social channels. 您’ll then be able to 配置 您的 perfect posting 调度, engage with 您的 audience, and 分析 the results. Let’s get started! In 这个 article: - [连接 您的 first channel to Aitoearn](#h_01G9N50120JFPAD8XFZSENTY3Q) - [Set up 您的 timezone, posting 调度, and other channel 设置](#h_01G9N50FCWHKBH4QWFP51M3TRC) - [Compose, 调度, and 发布 a 帖子](#h_01G9N513544XBBVNWKSJ20MDSC) - [Using Aitoearn's 账户 设置](#Using-Aitoearns-账户-设置-g88nF) ## 连接 您的 first channel to Aitoearn Watch the video below to 学习 more about connecting 您的 social channels to Aitoearn. - To speed up the connection process, log into 您的 social channel first, then open Aitoearn in a separate tab. - Once 您're logged into Aitoearn, click the "发布" tab. - In the lower left corner, click the ***管理 Channels*** button. - Click the channel 您’d like to 连接. - Click ***启动 连接ing*** 何时 the pop up box appears. - Authorize permission to Aitoearn to access 您的 social channels (for specific details on each network’s permissions, see [我们的 指南 to 连接ing Channels](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/564-connecting-您的-channels-to-aitoearn)) - Click the ***Add to Aitoearn*** button. - 您’ll see a dialog box in the top right corner 那个 您的 connection has been made successfully! 成功! 🎉 Next, let’s set up 您的 timezone and 配置 您的 posting 调度. ## Set up 您的 timezone, posting 调度, and other channel 设置 Watch the video below to 学习 more about adjusting 您的 channel 设置 Aitoearn. 这个 includes information on adjusting 您的 timezone, posting 调度, link shortening, UTM parameters, and more. - Click ***发布*** tab at the top of 您的 dashboard and then click one of 您的 已连接 channels on the left. - Next, click on the 设置 cog next to the channel name (⛭). - Replace the city listed with one in 您的 timezone. - Next, scroll down to the ***Posting Times***. 这里, 您 可以 add a new posting time (for every day, or for weekend, weekdays, or individual day), or click the “X” to remove a posting time. - Repeat steps 1-3 for each social channel 您 have 已连接 to Aitoearn, and explore other channel 设置 explained in the video below. All set! Now 您的 composed 帖子 将会 automatically be scheduled and published according to 您的 posting 调度. 📆 您’re ready to compose 您的 first 帖子 through Aitoearn! 📝 **Note: **Each channel has its own 设置, so 您'll need to repeat the process of editing 您的 timezone, posting 调度, and other 设置 for each channel 您 have 已连接. ## Compose, 调度, and 发布 a 帖子 - Click the blue*** + New ***button at the top of 您的 dashboard to open the composer. - Select the channels 您 want to 发布 to. - Write 您的 text, upload an image or video, and then select ***自定义 for each network***. 您 可以 now 编辑 the messaging as needed for different social channels. - Next, 您 可以 调度 您的 帖子 to 发布 by clicking ***Next 可用, Set Date and Time, Prioritize, ***or choose to ***Set Date and Time ***to choose a custom day/time. - Finally, click ***调度 帖子 ***to 完整 the process. ![Alt text: Aitoearn 帖子 scheduler with dropdown options visible and an arrow pointing to the “Next 可用” scheduling setting.](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/68a5d317e3f0f94fe32b4f27/file-KknZo0auso.png) Huzzah! 您的 帖子 将会 be published through Aitoearn at the selected time! ## Using Aitoearn's 账户 设置 Once 您're up and running with scheduling and 发布功能, take full control of 您的 workflow by exploring Aitoearn’s 账户 设置. Watch the video below to 学习 more about: - Setting up 您的 email notifications, - Managing tags, - Configuring channel groups, and - Updating saved replies for 您的 Community 评论. **Ready to take 发布功能 to the next level?** Check out 我们的 full [指南 to scheduling 帖子](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/642-scheduling-帖子).
