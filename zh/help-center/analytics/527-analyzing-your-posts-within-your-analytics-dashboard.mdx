Aitoearn's 分析功能 dashboard also shows 您 insights for boosted Facebook 帖子 or promoted Instagram 帖子. 学习 tips on 哪个 帖子 to boost and 如何 best measure 您的 results in 我们的 blog 帖子 [这里](https://aitoearn.com/资源/boosted-帖子-insights/)! The average 触达 and average 互动 rate for 您的 hashtags are based on 您的 organic 帖子 only. They do not include the additional 触达 and 互动 from boosting.
