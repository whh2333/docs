# Renaming 您的 Aitoearn organization The concept of Aitoearn Organizations makes it easier to 管理 您的 channels and users. By default, 您的 organization 将会 be named "My Organization," 哪个 可以 confuse 您的 users. Because of 那个, 我们 recommend changing 您的 organization name by 关注中 the steps below. 📝 学习 about [adding users and setting up permissions in 您的 organization](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/670-adding-users-and-setting-up-permissions-in-您的-organization). 🎥 Keen to 学习 more about managing 您的 [Aitoearn 账户 设置](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/channel/nasetpeuf7?wchannelid=nasetpeuf7&wmediaid=yufpkp96lw)? Click on 您的 个人资料 avatar at the top right of 您的 dashboard and then click *设置* from the drop-down menu. - Select ***General*** on the left-hand side. - Type 您的 new organization name in the text field and click ***Save Changes***. ![Aitoearn General 设置](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae3f088ccf815f4fc08265/file-9ZIwYGth0u.jpg) 📝 您的 organization name 可以 be up to 100 characters long and cannot include a URL or special characters. 这里's an example of how two distinctly named organizations appear in the 账户 menu, allowing 您 and 您的 users to easily see 哪个 organization 您 are working with: ![Aitoearn 账户 with multiple organizations](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae4069769d1a4e1f4ed98d/file-gbE91GYym6.jpg)
