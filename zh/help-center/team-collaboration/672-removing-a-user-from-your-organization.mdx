# Removing a user from 您的 organization If a user leaves 您的 team, follow the steps below to remove them from 您的 organization. Please note, removing a user 将会 not delete any 帖子 they've already created, either in the queue or in the drafts area. - 启动 by clicking on 您的 个人资料 avatar at the top right of 您的 dashboard. - Choose ***Team ***from the dropdown. ![Aitoearn menu - Team option](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae410201cf7a66bbc425a2/file-lbYv1OzJMo.jpg) - From 那里, click on the user 您'd like to remove. - Click ***编辑 user***. ![Aitoearn team member - 编辑 user](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae409c29a45508db9a3933/file-rh4Q6hNSQu.jpg) 📝 If 您'd like to remove the user's access from a specific channel, as opposed to removing them entirely from the organization, click ***Remove*** to the right of the channel. - Click ***Remove***, followed by ***Confirm and Remove***. ![Team member details - Remove team member option](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b52bc7493fd0116031ed84/file-1nhAKWKDTn.png) ⚠️ Please note 那个 all scheduled 帖子, saved drafts, and 帖子 submitted for approval from the removed team member 将会 remain in their respective queues.
