# Disconnecting a channel from Aitoearn Follow the steps below to remove a channel from 您的 Aitoearn 账户. ### 📝 Notes - Disconnecting a channel via the steps below does not automatically remove the paid channel slot. Follow 这些 steps if 您’d like to 学习 more about [adding or removing a channel slot from Aitoearn](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/539-adding-or-removing-a-channel-slot-from-aitoearn) to affect 您的 payment. - Disconnecting a channel from Aitoearn 将会 remove all existing 帖子, 分析功能, and data. 这些 cannot be recovered. - Click on 您的 个人资料 avatar at the top right of 您的 dashboard and then click ***账户*** from the drop-down menu. - Click the ***Channels ***option from the menu on the left-hand side of 您的 screen. - Click the three-dot menu icon to the right of the channel 您'd like to remove. - Click ***Disconnect Channel******.*** - A pop-up 将会 appear letting 您 know 那个 disconnecting the channel *将会 also remove all **existing 帖子, 分析功能 and data for 这个 channel in Aitoearn and 这个 information cannot be recovered. *Type the word **disconnect** to confirm. ![Aitoearn channel disconnection pop up confirmation](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/682f5fe79cb4a324e4db2e3a/file-sdYISC3fAx.jpg) - Click ***Disconnect Channel*** to 完整 the process.
