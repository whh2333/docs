 <h4 id="h_01HD29JGGP9F6GMATXQ4HSGPSB">üìù Good-to-Knows</h4>

  <li>When you change plans via the App store it takes effect immediately. You will receive a prorated refund for the existing plan and be charged immediately for the new plan with a new expiration date one month after you changed plans.</li>
 </ul>
</div>

<h2 id="h_01HD29JGGPP25KV38WMWX92TGT">iOS</h2>
<h3 id="h_01H6CGKMT7D413YCWFME2W7CAD">Starting a subscription</h3>
<ol>
 <li>From the Buffer app, tap the Settings wheel on the bottom right corner, then <em><strong>Account Settings.</strong></em></li>
 <li>Under the "Subscription" heading, tap <em><strong>View Plans</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Choose a plan from the options listed by tapping the purple <em><strong>Upgrade</strong></em> button.</li>
 <li>Confirm your purchase by double-clicking your side button as directed. You'll be charged on the card you have associated with the App Store.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="Restoring-a-subscription-gw0NB">Restoring a subscription</h3>
<p>If you have an existing subscription through the App Store that is not reflecting in the app, you can use the following steps to restore the subscription:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Open the Buffer mobile app.</li>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Settings</b></i> (gear icon) in the lower right corner of the app.</li>
 <li>Make sure the Account Settings tab at the top is selected.</li>
 <li>Scroll down until you see "Subscription" and then tap <i><b>Restore In-App Subscription.</b></i></li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01H6CGKZ48R4J51287C9MR8MYS">Upgrading or downgrading your subscription</h3>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üí° <strong>Not using the App Store? </strong>If your subscription wasn‚Äôt purchased through the App Store, <a href="#" rel="noopener">follow these steps to upgrade or downgrade on the web</a>.</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>From the Buffer app, tap the 3 bars in the top left corner.</li>
 <li>Scroll down and tap <em><strong>Add a new Channel</strong></em> to upgrade your plan.</li>
 <li>Or tap <em><strong>Adjust Plan</strong></em> to remove channels or downgrade your plan.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01GTVXMDSCG7YKYZCMRTMJTWFF">Cancelling your subscription</h3>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üìù <strong>Not subscribed through the mobile app? </strong>If your subscription wasn‚Äôt purchased through mobile, <a href="#" rel="noopener">follow these steps to cancel or delete your account</a>.</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>Go to the home screen of your iOS device and open the Settings app</li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Apple ID, iCloud, Media &amp; Purchases </strong></em><em>(listed under your name at top of the screen)</em></li>
 <li>Tap on <em><strong>Media &amp; Purchases</strong></em></li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>View Account</strong></em> and sign in</li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Subscriptions</strong></em>, and then tap <em><strong>Buffer</strong></em></li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Cancel Subscription</strong></em>, which will cancel the subscription at the end of the current billing cycle</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01GTVXMP2M01304XHY5WGY6PSY">Requesting a refund for incorrect charges</h3>
<p><b>From your iOS device:</b></p>
<ol>
 <li>Click here to request a refund: <a href="bufferapp://refund" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer ugc nofollow">bufferapp://refund</a>. (Note: this link only works on iOS devices)</li>
</ol>
<p><b>From a computer:</b></p>
<ol>
 <li>Visit Apple's "Report a Problem" page here from your computer: <a href="http://reportaproblem.apple.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">http://reportaproblem.apple.com/</a> for specific instructions.</li>
</ol>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üìù <b>Note:</b> Apple is only able to provide refunds for purchases made within the last 90 days.</p>
</div>
<h3 id="h_01GTVXMP2M01304XHY5WGY6PSY">Accessing receipts</h3>
<p><b>On iPhone</b></p>
<p>1. Open the App Store.</p>
<p>2. Tap your profile icon.</p>
<p>3. Tap your name at the top of the profile page.</p>
<p>4. Scroll down and choose Purchase History.</p>
<p>5. Find the order and tap the "Total Billed" label to see the transaction details.</p>
<p>6. Tap Resend to have it emailed to you again.</p>
<h2 id="h_01HD29JGGPGP5CEQTPW9HJC0HH">Android</h2>
<h3 id="h_01H6CHV80CR68NAD7PTSQBZYFF">Starting a subscription</h3>
<ol>
 <li>From the Buffer app, tap the Settings wheel on the bottom right corner, then <em><strong>Account Settings.</strong></em></li>
 <li>Under the "Subscription" heading, tap <em><strong>View Plans</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Choose a plan from the options listed by tapping the purple <em><strong>Upgrade</strong></em> button.</li>
 <li>Confirm your purchase. You'll be charged on the card you have associated with the Google Play.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="Restoring-a-subscription-pFh6w">Restoring a subscription</h3>
<p>If you have an existing subscription through the Google Play Store that is not reflecting in the app, you can use the following steps to restore the subscription:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Open Google Play Store app</li>
 <li>Tap profile icon &gt; "Payments &amp; subscriptions"</li>
 <li>Select "Subscriptions"</li>
 <li>Find the subscription you want to restore</li>
 <li>Tap "Reactivate" or "Restore"</li>
</ol>
<p>You can also find this option within the Buffer app:</p>
<ol>
 <li>From the Buffer app, tap the Settings wheel on the bottom right corner, then <em><strong>Account Settings.</strong></em></li>
 <li>Under the "Subscription" heading, tap <i><b>Restore In-App Subscription.</b></i></li>
</ol>

<h3 id="h_01H6CHVS4SBA5WAD7W3R1XWQC3">Upgrading or downgrading your subscription</h3>
<ol>
 <li>To upgrade, tap the three lines in the top left corner of your Buffer app and scroll down to select <em><strong>Add New Channel.</strong></em></li>
 <li>To change your plan or downgrade, select a channel from the left sidebar, then tap <em><strong>Account Settings.</strong></em> Scroll down to Subscriptions and tap <em><strong>Get More With Essentials</strong></em> to see all the plan options.</li>
 <li>Select your new plan from the menu.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01H6CHW355VY348WN480FF8CSQ">Cancelling your subscription</h3>
<ol>
 <li>On your mobile device, open the Google Play App.</li>
 <li>Tap your profile icon in the top right corner.</li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Payments and subscriptions</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Select Buffer from the list of subscriptions.</li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Cancel</strong></em> and follow the instructions provided onscreen.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01H6CHXVP10AQZWDNCV5T3FA6Y">Requesting a refund</h3>
<ol>
 <li>Head to Google Play's <a href="https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637?hl=en" target="_blank">Refund help page</a> for specific steps.</li>
</ol>

<p>2. Select ‚ÄúActivity‚Äù to display your Play Store order history</p>
<p>3. Choose an individual entry to view its corresponding receipt</p>
