<h2 id="h_d416f694-bb37-4b94-846e-dcb9775c2d3c">Making changes to your Buffer plan</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard and then click&nbsp;<i><b>Billing</b></i>.</li>
 <li>On the Billing screen, you have the following options for upgrading, making changes to billing details, and downloading invoices:</li>
</ol>
<ul>
 <li>Under&nbsp;<b>Plan,&nbsp;</b>you can:</li>
</ul>
<ol>
 <ul>
  <li><strong>Change Plan:</strong>&nbsp;Move from one plan to another.</li>
  <li><strong>Change Channel Quantity:*&nbsp;</strong>To add or remove the amount of paid channels from your plan, select the&nbsp;<strong><em>Add or Remove Channels</em></strong>&nbsp;button.</li>
  <li><strong>Cancel Plan:</strong>&nbsp;Cancel your paid subscription and downgrade to the Free plan at any time. Head here if you'd like to learn more about&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/541-how-to-cancel-your-subscription-or-delete-your-account" target="_self">How to cancel your subscription or delete your account</a>.</li>
 </ul>
</ol>
<p><img src="https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6807b7c081cf8731abca4d25/file-DqucQTfxJB.jpg" alt="Buffer Billing settings with an arrow pointing to the dropdown menu to Cancel Plan" style="max-width: 100%; "></p>
<ul>
 <li>Under&nbsp;<strong>Payment Method,&nbsp;</strong>you can:
  <ul>
   <li><strong>Update [or Add] Method:</strong>&nbsp;Add or update your payment method/credit card details.**</li>
  </ul></li>
 <li>Under&nbsp;<b>Additional Information:</b>
  <ul>
   <li>Update your billing address via&nbsp;<b>Update Information.</b></li>
   <li><b>Add your tax ID&nbsp;</b>if you're a business.</li>
  </ul></li>
 <li><strong>Invoices &amp; Receipts:</strong>&nbsp;View and download your invoices or receipts.</li>
</ul>
