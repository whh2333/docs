<h2 id="h_d7b56062-298b-40ae-9088-9133852addde">Cancelling your subscription</h2>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>The following steps are for cancelling subscriptions started on the desktop version of Buffer. For information on cancelling a mobile (in-app) subscriptions, please refer to <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/546-managing-subscriptions-from-the-app-or-google-play-store">Managing subscriptions from the App or Google Play Store</a>.</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard.</li>
 <li>Select <strong><em>Billing</em></strong> from the dropdown or <a href="https://account.buffer.com/billing">click this link</a>.</li>
 <li>Click the three vertical dots.</li>
 <li>Select <em><strong>Cancel Plan. </strong></em>Your plan will be active and open for use until the end of your billing period.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6807b7c081cf8731abca4d25/file-DqucQTfxJB.jpg" alt="Buffer Billing settings with an arrow pointing to the dropdown menu showing Cancel plan" style="max-width: 100%; "></p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üìù Once you cancel your subscription, you will still have access to our Free plan. Learn more about <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/597-what-happens-when-you-downgrade-to-the-free-plan" target="_self">what happens when you downgrade to the Free plan here</a>.</p>
</div>
