        <h1 class="title">Where to find your Buffer receipts and invoices</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>This guide outlines where to find your payment receipts or invoices for your Buffer subscription.</p>
<p>Once your payment goes through, we‚Äôll send you an automated email with a link to download your invoice. However, you can also download your receipt or invoice directly from your account.</p>
<p>Here's how to do this:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard and then select <strong><em>Billing</em></strong>.</li>
 <li>Scroll to the bottom of the page to find your <b>Invoices &amp; Receipts. </b></li>
 <li><p>Click <i><b>View </b></i>next to the charge, as shown below.</p>
  <figure style="width: 740px; " class=""><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/68934b717217d136bddb981e/file-VP0D94zkaC.jpg" alt="screenshot of the Invoices &amp; Receipts section of the Buffer Billing area">
   <figcaption>
    Image of Buffer's Invoices &amp; Receipts area
   </figcaption>
  </figure></li>
 <li>A new screen will appear and you'll then have the choice to <i><b>Download invoice </b></i>or <i><b>Download receipt.</b></i></li>
</ol>
