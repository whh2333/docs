        <h1 class="title">Using two-factor authentication in Buffer</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>Enabling two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your Buffer account. After entering your username and password to log into Buffer, you'll be asked for a second authentication code. Two-factor authentication codes can be generated via email or an authentication app, such as Google Authenticator or Authy (available on iOS and Android).&nbsp;<a href="https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/1066447?hl=en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Learn more about Google Authenticator here</a>.</p>
<p>In this article:</p>
<ol>
 <li><a href="#Enabling-two-factor-authentication-xRhA3">Enabling two-factor authentication</a></li>
 <li><a href="#Disabling-two-factor-authentication-yDZPu">Disabling two-factor authentication</a></li>
</ol>
<h2 id="Enabling-two-factor-authentication-xRhA3">Enabling two-factor authentication</h2>
<p>These are the steps you'll take to enable two-factor authentication for your account:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of&nbsp;dashboard, and click on&nbsp;<a href="https://publish.buffer.com/settings" target="_blank"><i><b>Settings</b></i></a><i><b>. </b></i></li>
 <li>Toggle "Two Factor Authentication" to&nbsp;<em><strong>Enabled </strong></em>(it will turn blue).</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/68404a8be042cf4da63a8dde/file-QRdT2LdkGs.jpg" alt="Buffer account settings with an arrow pointing to the toggle to enable two factor authentication" style="max-width: 100%; "></p>
<ol start="3">
 <li>Click&nbsp;<i><b>Email</b></i>&nbsp;or&nbsp;<em><strong>Authenticator App</strong></em>,&nbsp;depending on which method you'd prefer.</li>
</ol>
<ul>
 <li>If you choose to authenticate via email, you'll receive an email with a confirmation code. Enter the code in Buffer and click <i><b>Confirm. </b></i></li>
 <li>If you chose to authenticate via an authenticator app, open the app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) on your phone and scan the QR code. Then, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Next</strong></em>. Once scanned, your authenticator app will display a 6-digit code. Enter this code and click&nbsp;<em><strong>Next</strong></em>.</li>
</ul>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p><b>Note on authenticating via authenticator app: </b>If you lose your phone, you could get locked out of your Buffer account. Copy and save the one-time recovery code in a safe place so you can use it later if you're not able to log in with your phone.</p>
</div>
<ol start="3">
 <li>Finally, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Done</strong></em>,&nbsp;and you should be all set!</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="Disabling-two-factor-authentication-yDZPu">Disabling two-factor authentication</h2>
<p>Need to turn two-factor authentication off? You can do so with a few quick steps:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of&nbsp;dashboard, and click on&nbsp;<a href="https://publish.buffer.com/settings" target="_blank"><i><b>Settings</b></i></a><i><b>. </b></i></li>
 <li>Toggle "Two Factor Authentication" OFF.</li>
 <li><p>You'll be emailed a one time code.</p><p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/68404ad6e042cf4da63a8de0/file-1LQZklhNrw.jpg" alt="Confirmation pop-up to enter a code to disable 2FA" style="max-width: 100%; "></p></li>
 <li>Enter the code that's been emailed to you and click <i><b>Confirm </b></i>to complete the process.</li>
</ol>
