        <h1 class="title">Can I use Buffer in another language?</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>If you're wondering whether you can use Buffer in a language other than English, this article explains what's currently supported on both our web dashboard and mobile apps.</p>
<p>In this article:</p>
<ol>
 <li><a href="#Language-support-in-Buffers-web-dashboard-pNXYM" target="_blank">Language support in Buffer's web dashboard</a></li>
 <li><a href="#Language-support-in-Buffers-iOS-and-Android-mobile-apps---HeOk">Language support in Buffer's iOS and Android mobile apps </a></li>
</ol>
<h2 id="Language-support-in-Buffers-web-dashboard-pNXYM">Language support in Buffer's web dashboard</h2>
<p>Buffer's web dashboard at&nbsp;<a href="http://buffer.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">buffer.com</a>&nbsp;is only available in <b>English</b>. We do not have a way to translate the web interface into other languages. As such, Buffer does not support languages like French (Français), German (Deutsch), Chinese (中文, Zhōngwén), Japanese (日本語, Nihongo), Italian (Italiano), Arabic (العربية, al-‘Arabīyah), etc.</p>
<h2 id="Language-support-in-Buffers-iOS-and-Android-mobile-apps---HeOk">Language support in Buffer's iOS and Android mobile apps</h2>
<p>Buffer's iOS and Android apps are available in English or Spanish. You can change the language on our mobile app to Spanish by following these instructions: <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/638-using-the-buffer-mobile-app-in-another-language" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Using the Buffer mobile app in another language</a>. Our iOS and Android apps can't be translated into other languages, like French (Français), German (Deutsch), Chinese (中文, Zhōngwén), Japanese (日本語, Nihongo), Italian (Italiano), Arabic (العربية, al-‘Arabīyah), etc.</p>
