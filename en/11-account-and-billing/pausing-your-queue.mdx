        <h1 class="title">Pausing your queue</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>There are occasions when you might wish to pause your queue in order to stop posts being sent to your social media accounts. Buffer has a built-in pause feature allowing you to do just that!</p>
<p>When your queue is paused, all scheduled posts will also be paused and will not be sent out. Once your queue is unpaused, the posts you had scheduled will become active again and will be placed into the next available time slots (in the original order in which they were scheduled). You will also be able to reschedule any failed posts that were scheduled for a custom date and time.</p>

<h2 id="Pausing-your-queue-tzip8">Pausing your queue</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Choose&nbsp;<em><strong>Channels&nbsp;</strong></em>from the left side panel.</li>
 <li>Select the channel where you'd like to pause the queue.&nbsp;</li>
 <li><p>Click the gear icon next to the channel name<em><strong>.</strong></em></p><p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b65fa765e01777a933364f/file-Gn3mjulR7w.jpg" alt="Buffer channel - gear icon" style="width: 718.008px; "></p></li>
 <li>On the Settings Page, make sure the <i><b>General </b></i>tab is selected.</li>
 <li>Click the <i><b>Pause Queue </b></i>toggle to complete the process.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6759e0dac52c6c40b813decb/file-ByhfpSe9Ig.jpg" alt="General tab - Pause Queue toggled ON" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h2 id="Resuming-your-queue-aCfxx">Resuming your queue</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Select the paused channel on the left side of Buffer.</li>
 <li><p>Click the gear icon next to the channel name<em><strong>.</strong></em></p><p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b65fa765e01777a933364f/file-Gn3mjulR7w.jpg" alt="Buffer channel - gear icon" style="width: 100%; "></p></li>
 <li>On the Settings Page, make sure the <i><b>General </b></i>tab is selected.</li>
 <li>Click the <i><b>Pause Queue </b></i>toggle <b>OFF</b> to complete the process.</li>
</ol>
