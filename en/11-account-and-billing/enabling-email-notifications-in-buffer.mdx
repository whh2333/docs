<h2 id="Setting-default-timezone-for-notification-emails--ah1jT">Setting a default timezone for notification emails</h2>
<p>You can also set a global timezone within Buffer to make sure your email notifications get sent according to your timezone. Setting a global timezone will also ensure any new connected channels are set to the timezone as well.</p>
<p>To find this setting:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your Buffer dashboard.</li>
 <li>Choose <i><b>Settings.</b></i></li>
 <li>Click on <i><b>Preferences </b></i>on the left side of the page.</li>
 <li>Click on the timezone that's listed and search for a city in your timezone and select it.</li>
</ol>
