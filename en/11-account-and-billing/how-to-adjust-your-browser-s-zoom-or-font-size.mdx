        <h1 class="title">How to adjust your browser's zoom or font size</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>When viewing&nbsp;Buffer on the web, we recommend making sure your browser's zoom settings are set to 100%. If the zoom settings are set to something different, it can impact core functions of the dashboard, such as the ability to scroll through your queue and see posts scheduled further out into the future.&nbsp;</p>
<p>However, adjusting the zoom size within your browser could also impact the readability of the text on screen, so we've also included steps to adjust your browser's font below.</p>
<h2>Adjusting zoom settings in your browser</h2>
<p>To adjust the zoom settings in your browser, you can use the following tool tips:</p>
<ul>
 <li>Press&nbsp;<em><strong>Control-Plus sign(+)</strong></em>&nbsp;key on Windows PC</li>
 <li>Press<em><strong>&nbsp;Command-Plus sign(+)</strong></em>&nbsp; key on a Mac</li>
 <li>Press<em><strong>&nbsp;Control-Minus sign(-)</strong></em>&nbsp;&nbsp;key on Windows PC</li>
 <li>Press<em><strong>&nbsp;Command-Minus sign(-)</strong></em>&nbsp;key on a Mac</li>
</ul>
<h2>Adjusting your browser's font size</h2>
<p><strong>Chrome</strong></p>
<ol>
 <li>In the top right corner of Chrome, click the menu (three vertical dots)&nbsp;and choose&nbsp;<strong><em>Settings</em></strong>.</li>
 <li>Select&nbsp;<strong><em>Appearance</em></strong>&nbsp;on the left sidebar.</li>
 <li>Under&nbsp;<strong><em>Change font size,</em></strong>&nbsp;use the dropdown menu next&nbsp;to&nbsp;<em><strong>Font size</strong></em>&nbsp;to select the font size you want.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Firefox</strong></p>
<ol>
 <li>In the Menu bar at the top of the screen, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Firefox</strong></em>&nbsp;and select&nbsp;<em><strong>Preferences&nbsp;</strong></em>from the dropdown menu.&nbsp;</li>
 <li>In the&nbsp;<em><strong>General</strong></em>&nbsp;tab on the left sidebar, scroll down to&nbsp;<em><strong>Language and Appearance</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Under&nbsp;<em><strong>Fonts and Colors</strong></em>, use the drop-down menus to select the font and font size you prefer.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Safari</strong></p>
<ul>
 <li>To increase the font size, press&nbsp;<i><strong>Option</strong></i><i>-</i><em><strong>Command-Plus sign</strong></em><i><strong>&nbsp;(+)</strong></i></li>
 <li>To decrease the font size, press&nbsp;<strong><em>Option-Command-Minus sign</em></strong><strong>&nbsp;(-)</strong></li>
</ul>
