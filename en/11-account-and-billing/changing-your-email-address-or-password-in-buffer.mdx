<h2 id="h_01ERDDG2EZSQTEZGHDTC8B2A4M">Changing your email address or password</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard and then click&nbsp;<em><strong>Settings.</strong></em></li>
</ol>
<ul>
 <ul>
  <li><b>Changing email: </b>
   <ul>
    <li>Under "Email," enter the new email address or password that you'd like to use for your account. Click&nbsp;<em><strong>Save Changes.&nbsp;</strong></em></li>
    <li>A verification email (‚ö†Ô∏è action necessary) will be sent from&nbsp;hello@youraccount.buffer.com&nbsp;to the new email address and a notification email will be sent to your former email address. Please note that if this step is not completed, your email address will not change.</li>
   </ul></li>
  <li><b>Changing password: </b>
   <ul>
    <li>Under "Password," enter the new password you'd like to use for Buffer and click <i><b>Change Password.</b></i></li>
   </ul></li>
 </ul>
</ul>
