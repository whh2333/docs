        <h1 class="title">How locations work when scheduling Instagram posts through Buffer</h1>

        <div class="callout-red">
 <p>Adding an Instagram location is only available for:</p>
 <ul>
  <li><b>Instagram posts that are published automatically.</b> If your post is scheduled as a notification, the location field will only act as a placeholder to remind you of the location you‚Äôd like to manually add when publishing the post directly in the Instagram app.</li>
  <li><b>Instagram channels connected to Buffer through Facebook</b>. If you have connected Instagram to Buffer by logging into Instagram directly and want to add locations to your posts, you can <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/568-connecting-your-instagram-business-or-creator-account-to-buffer#Converting-and-linking-your-Instagram-account-to-a-Facebook-Page--TLG6Z">use these steps to connect a Facebook page and reauthenticate to Buffer through Facebook</a>.</li>
 </ul>
</div>
<p>Bring new eyes to your Instagram posts by adding a location. When you search for locations, we'll be able to tag any business or location that has an associated Facebook page.</p>
<p>Start typing in the name of the Facebook Page associated in the location field of Buffer. If the Facebook Page has a location set up, it'll populate in your search results.</p>
