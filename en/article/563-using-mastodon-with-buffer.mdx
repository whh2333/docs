<h2 id="01H85TJYNSGGQWBYQA6YDR24F0">Connecting and refreshing your Mastodon channel</h2>
<div class="callout-red">
 <p>Connecting Mastodon works slightly differently from other channels. Because Mastodon is decentralized, you'll need to join a server before you can connect Mastodon to Buffer.</p>
</div>
<h3>Join a Mastodon server</h3>
<ol>
 <li>Find the address of a server running the Mastodon software (called an&nbsp;<i>Instance</i>) that is accepting new sign-ups to join.
  <ul>
   <li>At Buffer, we chose the Mastodon World server, and you're welcome to&nbsp;<a href="https://mastodon.world/invite/iUGVy5pH?ref=buffer.com">join us there</a> or you can also out&nbsp;<a href="https://joinmastodon.org/servers?ref=buffer.com">Mastodon's servers list</a>&nbsp;for potential servers to join.</li>
  </ul></li>
 <li>Once you identify an instance you would like to join that‚Äôs also accepting new members, fill in the form and choose an ID.</li>
 <li>Click <i><b>Sign Up</b></i> and wait for the confirmation email.</li>
 <li>Once you've received confirmation, you can proceed to <a href="#Connecting-your-Mastodon-channel-to-Buffer-s7XVN">connecting your Mastodon channel to Buffer.</a></li>
</ol>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>Check out our <a href="https://buffer.com/resources/mastodon-social/#step-2-sign-up-to-your-chosen-mastodon-server">Beginner's Guide to Mastodon</a> for more tips on joining a server and setting up your Mastodon profile.</p>
</div>
<h3 id="Connecting-your-Mastodon-channel-to-Buffer-s7XVN">Connecting your Mastodon channel to Buffer</h3>
<div class="callout-red">
 <p>If you wish to connect multiple Mastodon accounts to Buffer, you'll need to open a new tab and make sure you are logged into the Mastodon server with the account you wish to connect to Buffer before going through the steps below.</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>Head to&nbsp;<a href="https://account.buffer.com/channels" target="_blank">https://account.buffer.com/channels</a>&nbsp;</li>
 <li>Select Mastodon.</li>
 <li>Select the server you signed up with. You'll be asked to sign into your account to connect it to Buffer.</li>
</ol>
<p>To see a step-by-step guide and troubleshooting steps, check out the article:&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/564-connecting-your-channels-to-buffer" target="_self">Connect your channels to Buffer</a>.</p>

<h2 id="h_01H7JKXN8DH37DZAFKAC1ZGY0X">Engaging with Mastodon posts</h2>
<p>Buffer‚Äôs engagement features are only available for Facebook and Instagram. Mastodon is not supported within Buffer's engagement features at this time.</p>
