<h2 id="Changing-your-Buffer-account-avatar-or-logo-zr6d4">Changing your Buffer account avatar or logo</h2>
<p>When you create a Buffer account, you can upload your own photo or your company logo within Buffer's <b>Profile </b>settings.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/65b2fbb620e3b82eb738df96/file-koG7p4QsIb.jpg" alt="Buffer profile icon" style="width: 350.977px; "></p>
<p>To do this:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click the profile icon in the upper right corner of the web dashboard.</li>
 <li>Select <b><i>Settings </i></b>from the dropdown.</li>
 <li>From here, you can click <i><b>Upload Photo </b></i>to add your image.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae761779ed503a740ba418/file-jx92aH1Z5l.jpg" alt="Buffer - upload photo" style="max-width: 100%; "></p>
<h2 id="Changing-your-Buffer-user--account-name-thUA3">Changing your Buffer user / account name</h2>
<p>Your username will appear on all posts and drafts you create. To change your name, you can take the following steps:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Click the profile icon in the upper right corner of the web dashboard.</li>
 <li>Select <b><i>Settings</i></b> from the dropdown.</li>
 <li>Update the <b>Name </b>field with your preferred name and click <i><b>Save Changes. </b></i></li>
</ol>
