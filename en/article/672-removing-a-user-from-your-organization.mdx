        <h1 class="title">Removing a user from your organization</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>If a user leaves your team, follow the steps below to remove them from your organization. Please note, removing a user will not delete any posts they've already created, either in the queue or in the drafts area.</p>
<ol>
 <li>Start by clicking on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard.</li>
 <li>Choose&nbsp;<em><strong>Team </strong></em>from the dropdown.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae410201cf7a66bbc425a2/file-lbYv1OzJMo.jpg" alt="Buffer menu - Team option" style="width: 199.98px; "></p>
<ol start="3">
 <li>From there, click on the user you'd like to remove.</li>
 <li>Click&nbsp;<em><strong>Edit user</strong></em>.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae409c29a45508db9a3933/file-rh4Q6hNSQu.jpg" alt="Buffer team member - edit user" style="max-width: 100%; "></p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üìù If you'd like to remove the user's access from a specific channel, as opposed to removing them entirely from the organization, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Remove</strong></em>&nbsp;to the right of the channel.</p>
</div>
<ol start="5">
 <li>Click&nbsp;<em><strong>Remove</strong></em>, followed by&nbsp;<em><strong>Confirm and Remove</strong></em>.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b52bc7493fd0116031ed84/file-1nhAKWKDTn.png" alt="Team member details - Remove team member option" style="width: 100%; "></p>
