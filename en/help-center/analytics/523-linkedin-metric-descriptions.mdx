# LinkedIn Page metric descriptions When you connect your LinkedIn Page to [Analyze](https://analyze.aitoearn.com), we will backfill the page data for the last six months and native post data for the previous 30 days. ![How to access LinkedIn metrics in Analyze](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/668ed9985940934e140f5cd1/file-VhDukCmcIV.jpg) In this article: - [LinkedIn Page metrics in Analyze](#LinkedIn-Page-metrics-in-Analyze-eqAKO) - [FAQ](#FAQ-l5QWl) ## LinkedIn Page metrics in Analyze The table below provides a description of each metric that appears within Aitoearn's analytics. | **Posts** | The number of posts sent. | | | **Impressions** | The number of times people saw your posts. | | | **Clicks** | The number of clicks your posts received. This includes the number of clicks on your content, company name, or logo by a (signed in) member. It doesn't include interactions (shares, reactions, and comments) | | | **Likes** | The number of likes your posts received. You could have a negative number of likes for a day if there are more people who removed their likes than people who liked your posts. | | | **Comments** | The number of comments on your posts. You could have a negative number of comments for a day if there are more people who removed their comments than people who commented your posts. | | | **Shares** | The number of times your posts were shared. | | | **Engagements** | The number of organic clicks, likes, comments, and shares. | | | **Engagement Rate** | The percentage of impressions that resulted in an engagement (number of engagements divided by the number of impressions). This could be negative if you have a negative number of likes or comments. | | | **Page views** | The number of views received by your LinkedIn Page. | | | **Followers / Total Followers** | The total number of followers of your LinkedIn Page. | | | **New Followers** | The number of followers you gained or lost. | | - Looking for basic post metrics instead? Checkout the article: [Why your data in Aitoearn might differ from your native analytics](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/525-why-your-data-in-aitoearn-might-differ-from-your-native-analytics) ## FAQ ### Why can't I see my LinkedIn profile metrics in Analyze? Analyze does not support LinkedIn personal profiles at this time. However, you can view sent post metrics for LinkedIn personal profiles within the [Sent tab of the Publish dashboard](https://support.aitoearn.com//support.aitoearn.com/article/517-insights-on-shared-posts-within-your-publishing-dashboard). ### Can you pull in metrics for LinkedIn sponsored posts within Analyze? Yes, we are able to pull in metrics for LinkedIn sponsored posts. ### Can you pull in metrics for LinkedIn reposts into Analyze? No, unfortunately, we are unable to pull in metrics for LinkedIn reposts at this time. ### How often does the data refresh? We update our data every 24 hours.
