## Common Error Categories

### Authentication Errors
- **Invalid credentials** - Username/password issues
- **Expired tokens** - Refresh connection requirements
- **Permission denied** - Account access problems
- **Two-factor authentication** - 2FA-related issues

### Publishing Errors
- **Post failed to publish** - Content delivery problems
- **Character limit exceeded** - Text length issues
- **Invalid media format** - Image/video format problems
- **Duplicate content** - Repeat post detection

### Connection Errors
- **Channel unavailable** - Account connection issues
- **Rate limiting** - API usage restrictions
- **Network timeouts** - Connectivity problems
- **Server errors** - Platform downtime

### Content Errors
- **Media upload failed** - File size or format issues
- **Link sharing blocked** - URL restriction problems
- **Hashtag limitations** - Platform-specific hashtag rules
- **Mention restrictions** - Tagging limitations

## Quick Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Check platform status** - Verify if the social platform is experiencing issues
2. **Refresh connection** - Update your channel connection in Aitoearn
3. **Verify content** - Ensure your post meets platform requirements
4. **Review permissions** - Check account access and permissions
5. **Contact support** - Reach out if issues persist

## Error Resolution Process

When you encounter an error:

1. **Note the error message** - Copy the exact error text
2. **Check the error library** - Search for your specific error
3. **Follow troubleshooting steps** - Apply suggested solutions
4. **Test the fix** - Try posting again
5. **Document the solution** - Keep notes for future reference

## Getting Additional Help

If you can't find your specific error or the suggested solutions don't work:

- **Contact support** - Email us at [hello@aitoearn.com](mailto:hello@aitoearn.com)
- **Include details** - Provide the error message, platform, and steps taken
- **Share screenshots** - Visual information helps our team assist you faster
- **Mention timing** - Let us know when the error occurred

## Prevention Tips

- **Keep connections fresh** - Regularly refresh your channel connections
- **Follow platform guidelines** - Adhere to each platform's content policies
- **Monitor account health** - Check for warnings or restrictions on your social accounts
- **Stay updated** - Keep informed about platform changes and updates
