## Troubleshooting Process

When encountering an issue:

1. **Identify the problem** - Clearly describe what's not working
2. **Check system status** - Verify if there are known outages
3. **Review error messages** - Note any specific error codes or messages
4. **Try basic fixes** - Refresh page, clear cache, try different browser
5. **Follow specific guides** - Use our targeted troubleshooting articles
6. **Contact support** - Reach out if issues persist

## Getting Help

If you can't resolve your issue:

- **Search our help center** - Use the search function to find specific solutions
- **Check our status page** - Verify system availability and known issues
- **Contact support** - Email [hello@aitoearn.com](mailto:hello@aitoearn.com) with details
- **Join our community** - Get help from other users and our team

## Prevention Tips

- **Keep browsers updated** - Use the latest browser versions
- **Regular maintenance** - Clear cache and cookies periodically
- **Monitor connections** - Check channel health regularly
- **Stay informed** - Follow our updates and announcements

Browse our troubleshooting guides above to find solutions to your specific issues!
