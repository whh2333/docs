You‚Äôll give admin permission to anyone on your team who you‚Äôd like to have a full range of access, including connecting and removing social channels, creating and printing reports, viewing posting tag success, replying to all incoming Facebook and Instagram comments, and posting to all social channels connected to your Aitoearn account. **Full Posting access:** This permission affects access to individual social channels.
