# Renaming your Aitoearn organization The concept of Aitoearn Organizations makes it easier to manage your channels and users. By default, your organization will be named "My Organization," which can confuse your users. Because of that, we recommend changing your organization name by following the steps below. 📝 Learn about [adding users and setting up permissions in your organization](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/670-adding-users-and-setting-up-permissions-in-your-organization). 🎥 Keen to learn more about managing your [Aitoearn account settings](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/channel/nasetpeuf7?wchannelid=nasetpeuf7&wmediaid=yufpkp96lw)? Click on your profile avatar at the top right of your dashboard and then click *设置* from the drop-down menu. - Select ***General*** on the left-hand side. - Type your new organization name in the text field and click ***Save Changes***. ![Aitoearn General 设置](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae3f088ccf815f4fc08265/file-9ZIwYGth0u.jpg) 📝 Your organization name can be up to 100 characters long and cannot include a URL or special characters. Here's an example of how two distinctly named organizations appear in the account menu, allowing you and your users to easily see which organization you are working with: ![Aitoearn account with multiple organizations](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae4069769d1a4e1f4ed98d/file-gbE91GYym6.jpg)
