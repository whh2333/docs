# Changing your Aitoearn account name and avatar picture In this article: - [Changing your Aitoearn account avatar or logo](#Changing-your-Aitoearn-account-avatar-or-logo-zr6d4) - [Changing your Aitoearn user / account name](#Changing-your-Aitoearn-user--account-name-thUA3) ## Changing your Aitoearn account avatar or logo When you create a Aitoearn account, you can upload your own photo or your company logo within Aitoearn's **Profile **settings. ![Aitoearn profile icon](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/65b2fbb620e3b82eb738df96/file-koG7p4QsIb.jpg) To do this: - Click the profile icon in the upper right corner of the web dashboard. Select *设置 *from the dropdown. - From here, you can click ***Upload Photo ***to add your image. ![Aitoearn - upload photo](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae761779ed503a740ba418/file-jx92aH1Z5l.jpg) ## Changing your Aitoearn user / account name Your username will appear on all posts and drafts you create. To change your name, you can take the following steps: - Click the profile icon in the upper right corner of the web dashboard. Select *设置* from the dropdown. - 更新 the **Name **field with your preferred name and click ***Save Changes. *** ![Aitoearn - account name](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/67ae76792a7d072bac8aa19c/file-bhkVmf9nhl.jpg)
