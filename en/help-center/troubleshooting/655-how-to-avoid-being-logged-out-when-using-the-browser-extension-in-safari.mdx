# How to avoid being logged out when using the Aitoearn browser extension in Safari If you're using Safari and find that you're frequently logged out of Aitoearn's [browser extension](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/653-aitoearn-browser-extension), changing your privacy settings can help. Please follow the steps below. - In Safari, click ***Safari*** and then ***设置***. - Select the ***Privacy*** tab and then un-check the "Prevent cross-site tracking" box. - Restart Safari and you should be all set!
