<h2 id="Common-connection-issues-R4gAZ">Troubleshooting common connection errors</h2>
<h3 id="Error-Weve-hit-a-snag-uwkg8">Error: <i>We've hit a snag!</i></h3>
<p>This error can come up for a few reasons:</p>
<ul>
 <li>If your Instagram account is new and you have not completed the "setup" steps in Instagram.
  <ul>
   <li>Please check your account and make sure to complete all setup steps, such as adding a bio and profile picture.</li>
  </ul></li>
 <li>If you cancel the connection flow mid-way and try to connect the channel again. 
  <ul>
   <li>To fix this, please try <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/508-opening-buffer-in-an-incognito-or-private-window-how-to-safelist-buffer-and-using-a-vpn">opening Buffer in a private browser window</a> and then try connecting Instagram again, making sure to go through all steps in the connection process.</li>
  </ul></li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Error-errortype-OAuthException-code-400-errormessage--vu6Yc">Error: <i>{"error_type": "OAuthException", "code": 400, "error_message": "Your activity off Meta technologies is currently turned off."} </i></h3>
<ol>
 <li>Log into the account on Instagram.com.</li>
 <li>Visit <a href="https://accountscenter.instagram.com/info_and_permissions/off_facebook_activity/?entry_point=ACCOUNTS_CENTER">https://accountscenter.instagram.com/info_and_permissions/off_facebook_activity/?entry_point=ACCOUNTS_CENTER</a></li>
 <li>A new page will appear, and you will select "Manage Future Activity."</li>
 <li>In order for things to work properly, the option for "Connect Future Activity" would need to be selected (<a href="https://share.buffer.com/NQu9yNjJ">screenshot</a>).</li>
 <li>Once you've gone through this steps, please try connecting Instagram to Buffer again.</li>
</ol>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>Note: you may need to repeat these steps for any other Instagram account you are trying to connect to Buffer.</p>
</div>
<h3 id="Error-Failed-to-get-instagram-login-credentials-Request-failed-with--i2-sW">Error: <i>Failed to get instagram login credentials: Request failed with status code 400 (Mobile error)</i></h3>
<p>This error can appear in the mobile app if your Instagram account is new and you have not completed the "setup" steps in Instagram.</p>
<ul>
 <li>Please check your account and make sure to complete all setup steps, such as adding a bio and profile picture.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Error-Invalid-session-error-400-2bvJG">Error: <i>Invalid session error 400</i></h3>
<p>This is a known issue that Meta is working to resolve.</p>

<p>In some cases, customers have been able to connect Instagram by waiting a few hours and trying again.</p>
<p>Otherwise, you can try connecting to Buffer by linking your Instagram account to a Facebook page and <a href="#Connecting-your-Professional-Instagram-account-through-a-linked-Facebo-HPnL-" target="_blank">following these steps to authorize Buffer</a>.</p>
