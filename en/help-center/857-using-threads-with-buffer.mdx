Topics act as content labels that group related posts together in Threads, making it easier for followers to explore specific themes or conversations.</p> <p>You can add a topic in one of two ways when scheduling a Threads post in Buffer.</p> <h3 id="Option-1-Add-a-topic-in-the-Topic-field-Recommended-u5m4D">Option 1: Add a topic in the "Topic" field (Recommended)</h3> <div class="callout-blue"> <p>To ensure your topic is applied correctly, we recommend using the <strong>Topic</strong> field in the composer. It gives you full control and ensures consistency across your scheduled content.</p> </div> <p>In the Buffer post composer, add the topic you want to add (without the hashtag symbol) for the <strong>Topic</strong> field. That's it!</p> <img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6881023b401e5f22412d7066/file-lSAzM70nHy.jpg" style="width: 550px; " /> <h3 id="Option-2-Use-an-in-text-topic-tag-ZFl8V">Option 2: Use an in-text topic tag</h3> <p>You can also add a topic by including a valid tag within the body of your post.</p> <img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/688101e8c11e7d1454719293/file-iulU0BKSxh.jpg" alt="Image of Buffer composer with an inline topic (#TennisThreads) added to the post. Arrows pointing to the hashtag in the post and in the post preview." style="width: 492px; " /> <p><strong>Important rules for in-text tags:</strong></p> <ul> <li></li><p>The tag must start with a <strong>hashtag symbol (</strong><code class="inline-code"><strong>#</strong></code> <strong> )</strong>.</p><p><strong>Only one topic tag</strong> is allowed per post. Buffer will use the <strong>first valid</strong> one found.</p><p>The tag will appear without the <code class="inline-code">#</code> in the Buffer dashboard.</p><p><strong>Tags with numbers only</strong>, like <code class="inline-code">#1</code> , will be ignored (assumed to be number signs).</p><p>In-text tags will automatically <strong>end before</strong> any of the following:</p> <ul> <li>Spaces or line breaks</li> <li>Periods </li><code class="inline-code">.</code></li> <li>Ampersands </li><code class="inline-code">&amp;</code></li> <li>At signs </li><code class="inline-code">@</code></li> <li>Exclamation marks </li><code class="inline-code">!</code></li> <li>Question marks </li><code class="inline-code">?</code></li> <li>Commas </li><code class="inline-code">,</code></li> <li>Semi-colons </li><code class="inline-code">;</code></li> <li>Colons </li><code class="inline-code">:</code></li> </ul></li> </ul> <p><strong>Example:</strong></p> <ul> <li>‚úÖ </li><b>What changes have you made to your practice that improved your #Tennis serve? </b>(correct)</li> <li>üö´ </li><b>The #1 tip I recommend is... </b> (incorrect - the "#1" won‚Äôt be treated as a topic)</li> </ul> <h2 id="01H85TJYNSH5M13KP58PGVSJY3">Image and video specifications for Threads</h2> <p>Threads supports images and videos.
