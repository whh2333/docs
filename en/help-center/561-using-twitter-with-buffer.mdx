<h2 id="h_01H6SJ4RZRM2FSJ4QJ4DERQP00">Connecting and refreshing your X/Twitter profile</h2>
<p>Head to your Channels area to connect your X/Twitter account:&nbsp;<a href="https://account.buffer.com/channels">https://account.buffer.com/channels</a></p>
<p>You can connect multiple X/Twitter accounts to Buffer. To make sure Buffer knows which one want to connect, log in to that X/Twitter account in another browser tab before trying to connect it to Buffer.</p>
<p>To see a step-by-step guide and troubleshooting steps for connecting X/Twitter, check out the article:&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/564-connecting-your-channels-to-buffer" target="_blank">Connect your channels to Buffer</a>.</p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <ul>
  <li><a href="https://help.twitter.com/en/managing-your-account/how-to-use-the-delegate-feature"><b>X/Twitter‚Äôs Delegate feature</b></a> is not supported at this time. To connect your X/Twitter profile to Buffer, you‚Äôll need to be logged into X.com/Twitter.com as the owner of the X/Twitter profile.</li>
  <li><b>Connecting multiple X/Twitter accounts - </b>Buffer will automatically try to connect to whichever account you're logged into in your browser, so we recommend logging into <a href="http://X.com/">X.com/</a> <a href="http://Twitter.com">Twitter.com</a> with the account you want to connect to Buffer first and then heading to Buffer to connect the channel.</li>
 </ul>
</div>
<p>If your connected X/Twitter channel requires a refresh, check out the article&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/573-refreshing-a-channel-in-buffer" target="_blank">Refreshing a channel in Buffer</a>.</p>

<h4 id="01H83YSYPMJWWNBHM6KPDBGFFP">Note for GIFs:</h4>
<p>If you see quality loss or errors when creating posts&nbsp;with animated GIFs, try these recommendations to improve success:</p>
<ul>
 <li>GIF Maximum resolution - 1280 x 1080 (width x height)</li>
 <li>GIF Maximum number of frames - 350</li>
 <li>GIF Maximum number of pixels (width * height * num_frames) - 300 million</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="01H83VZDBRFQ45TAVA5G2C3S7S">Engagement on X/Twitter posts</h2>
<p>At this time, Buffer's engagement features are only available for Facebook and Instagram. X/Twitter is not supported within Buffer's engagement features at this time.</p>
<h2 id="01H83ZABE2286HH3G3FQF7RZ6X">X/Twitter analytics</h2>
<div class="callout-red">
 <p>X/Twitter analytics are available on our <a href="http://buffer.com/pricing" target="_blank">paid plans</a>.</p>
</div>
<p>We can post all day, but the real excitement comes when seeing the reactions to our posts. If you‚Äôre on the Essentials or Team plans, you‚Äôll find all of your stats within two places: the Sent tab of our Publish dashboard offers basic post metrics while our Analytics dashboard offers advanced analytics.</p><br>
<h3>Basic analytics</h3>
<p>To get a quick view of each of your X/Twitter account's metrics, head to the <b>Publish</b> tab of the web dashboard and then click the <b>Sent</b> tab to access likes, clicks, reposts, and impressions.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b67d2e48915f570c6a93ed/file-fkgXxIfbdf.jpg" alt="X Sent Posts" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h3>Advanced Analytics</h3>
<p>For a more in-depth look at how your X/Twitter posts are performing, head to the <b>Analyze</b> tab of the web dashboard. To dive straight in, learn about our&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/522-twitter-metric-descriptions" target="_blank">X/Twitter metric descriptions</a>, select X/Twitter on the left-hand side of your analytics dashboard, and peruse the tabs:</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/667c60f35940934e140f535e/file-0dJbpBzk0c.jpg" alt="Buffer Analyze Overview screen with arrows pointing to Analyze and Twitter/X" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Overview</strong>: gives insight into your overall engagement levels for your profile over time. Learn more about the <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/526-performance-overview" target="_blank">Performance overview on your analytics dashboard</a>.</li>
 <li><strong>Posts</strong>: provides a history of all of your published posts and how they are performing. Learn more about <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/527-analyzing-your-posts-within-your-analytics-dashboard" target="_blank">Analyzing your posts within your analytics dashboard</a>.</li>
