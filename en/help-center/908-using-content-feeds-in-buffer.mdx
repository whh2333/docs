Feeds refresh live, pulling directly from source websites.</p> <h3>How often do feeds refresh?</h3> <p>Feeds refresh automatically when you access them in Buffer, pulling the latest available articles.</p> <h3>How are 'Explore Feeds' chosen?</h3> <p>'Explore feeds' selected based on popularity and usefulness, manually curated by our team. There‚Äôs no automated recommendation at this time.</p> <h3>Why is my feed not loading or why is my feed showing errors?</h3> <p>Check the feed URL for accuracy or test it in an external RSS validator tool. Feeds must be RSS or Atom formats.</p> <ul> <li>Here are a few resources to check the feed URL: </li><ul> <li></li><a href="https://validator.w3.org/feed/">https://validator.w3.org/feed/</a></li> <li></li><a href="https://getrssfeed.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">https://getrssfeed.com/</a></li> <li></li><a href="https://rssfinder.app/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">https://rssfinder.app/</a></li> </ul></li> </ul> <h3>Why isn‚Äôt a specific article showing in my feed?</h3> <p>Buffer displays exactly what the RSS source provides. If an article isn't appearing, it's typically because the site itself hasn't included it in their RSS.</p> </article>
