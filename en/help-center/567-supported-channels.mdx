# Supported channels <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a> <div class="callout-red"> <p>**📝 What counts as a channel in Buffer?**</p> Each social account you connect is a channel. For instance, a Facebook Page, Facebook Group, and LinkedIn Profile would count as 3 channels. <p>**📝 Can I connect multiple accounts from the same social network?**</p> Yes! Got 5 Facebook accounts? You can connect all of them to your Buffer account. This applies to all of our supported channels! </div> <div class="callout-blue"> #### Notes - Unsure how to connect your channels? Checkout [Connecting your channels to Buffer](https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/564-connecting-your-channels-to-buffer).</li> - Due to API restrictions, it's not possible to connect Instagram personal profiles and Facebook personal profiles to Buffer, along with LinkedIn groups and Pinterest personal accounts. - Analytics are not available for Facebook stories, IGTV, or IG Reels due to API limitations. - If your channel is not appearing in Analyze or Engage, it’s possible that it is an unsupported channel.
