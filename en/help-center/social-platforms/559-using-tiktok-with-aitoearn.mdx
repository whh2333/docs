## Video specs (summary)

- Portrait 9:16 (1080×1920) recommended; also 16:9 or 1:1
- Formats: MP4 / MOV; up to ~1 GB (≤300 MB recommended)
- Typical duration: up to 10–15 minutes depending on account
- Custom cover supported; add tags/topics
