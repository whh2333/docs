It only re-authorizes the application to post for you.</p> <h3>Will Buffer automatically retry my failed posts once I refresh the connection?</h3> <p>No, Buffer will not automatically retry your failed posts if the connection fails. Therefore, once you refresh your connection, we recommend retrying any failed posts that you would like to publish.</p> </article>
