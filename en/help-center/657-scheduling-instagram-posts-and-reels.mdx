<h2 id="Automatic-vs-notification-publishing---which-is-right-for-my-post-21sNu">Automatic vs. notification publishing - which is right for my post?</h2>
<div>
 Certain features (like scheduling a first comment) are available when publishing automatically to Instagram Professional accounts, while others (like adding music to a reel or stickers to a story) can only be added if you are scheduling to an Instagram Personal account or you set a notification and finish the post yourself on Instagram. <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/554-using-instagram-with-buffer#h_01HB99SMK6389CZEMAG1XBP6K6">Learn more about how Buffer works on Instagram Personal accounts vs. Professional accounts here</a>.
</div>
<div>
 Every time you create a post, story, or reel in Buffer, you can choose one of the following options:
</div>
<div>
 <b>Automatic publishing</b>
</div>
<div>
 Set it and forget it. Buffer will automatically post on your behalf so that you can focus on other things, like replying to comments or creating new content.
</div>
<div>
 <b>Notification publishing</b>
</div>
<div>
 Add story stickers, music, product tags, story links, and enjoy more creative control. Buffer will set up your post, send you a notification, and help you finish the post yourself on Instagram.
</div>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>If the image or video within your post falls outside of <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/622-instagrams-accepted-aspect-ratio-ranges">Instagram's accepted aspect ratio ranges</a>, it cannot be scheduled for automatic publishing and will be scheduled as a notification instead.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="01H7G1SZ6VRWNEKE8VK4AQGP15">Creating your Instagram post, reel, or story&nbsp;</h2>

 <li>To get started, select your Instagram account on the left-hand side of your dashboard.</li>
 <li>Open the composer at the top of your queue.</li>
 <li>Choose to schedule your post as a&nbsp;<em><strong>Post</strong></em><em>,&nbsp;</em><em><strong>Reel</strong></em><em>,&nbsp;</em>or <em><strong>Story</strong></em><em>.</em></li>

 <ul>
  <li>Videos must be 100 MB or smaller.</li>
  <li>Instagram creator accounts cannot schedule Instagram stories for automatic publishing. Instead, they'll need to be scheduled as a notification.</li>
  <li>Instagram carousel can only contain images at this time. Carousel posts do not support mixed media, meaning you will not be able to create a post containing both an image and video or a post containing multiple videos.</li>

  <li>If you are trying to schedule an Instagram story with multiple videos or images, you will need to schedule each image or video as a separate story in Buffer.</li>
  <li>If your image or video falls outside of&nbsp;<a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/hc/en-us/articles/360037965494">Instagram's accepted aspect ratio range</a>, it will get scheduled as a notification and will need to be posted manually.&nbsp;<a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039027053">Learn more about how to finish publishing using notifications here.</a></li>

<h2 id="How-to-add-a-thumbnail-to-your-video-Cy5lJ">How to add a thumbnail to your video</h2>
<p>By default, the first frame of the video (0 seconds) will be used as the thumbnail. If you'd like to change your thumbnail, you'll follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Once your video has finished uploading, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Edit Thumbnail</strong></em><em>,</em></li>
 <li>Drag the slider to the position in the video you'd like to use as the cover/thumbnail image.</li>
 <li>Click&nbsp;<em><strong>Save</strong></em>.</li>
</ol>

<div class="callout-red">

<h2 id="FAQ-g6ioI">FAQ</h2>
<h3>Is it possible to hide the number of likes from Instagram posts or reels?</h3>
<p>No, at this time, it is not possible to hide the number of likes from your posts while scheduling your content in Buffer. To hide the number of likes on your post, you can view the published post on Instagram and use the <i><b>Hide Likes</b></i> feature on the post.</p>
<h3>Will Instagram posts sent through Buffer be automatically published to Facebook if I have this setting enabled within Instagram?</h3>
<p>Unfortunately, due to API limitations, Instagram posts that are published through Buffer will not automatically publish to Facebook, even if this setting is enabled on your Instagram account. To have an Instagram post automatically publish to Facebook, you would need to create the post directly on Instagram.</p>
<h3>Is it possible to move the position of my text or change the font of my Instagram story or reel?</h3>
<p>No, it is not possible to move the position of your Instagram text or change the font in Buffer. Due to API limitations, Buffer does not support editing the font or moving the text on the screen for Instagram Reels directly within the app. The preview you see in Buffer is just a guideline so you have an idea of what your text&nbsp;<i>could&nbsp;</i>look like.</p>
<p>To customize the font and position of your text, you'll need to do so within the Instagram app itself. You can schedule your Reel with Buffer using the&nbsp;<i><b>Notify Me</b></i>&nbsp;option, and when it's time to post, you'll receive a notification on your phone. You can then open Instagram and add your text, choosing the font and positioning it as you like.</p>
