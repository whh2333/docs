<article id="fullArticle"> <h1 class="title">Scheduling reposts/retweets</h1> <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a> <p>Spacing out your posts is a great way to avoid flooding your X/Twitter feed. Adding reposts to your queue within Buffer allows you to share content from your community and ensures you're still providing great content to your followers, all within your preset schedule.</p> <p>In this article:</p> <ol> <li></li><a href="#h_01H6R788W7Z5DJAGKSZSHWXH3X" target="_self">Schedule Repost on a web browser</a></li> <li></li><a href="#h_01H6R8JDWZNJS07A2MS2P6XCDR" target="_self">Schedule Retweet on the mobile app</a></li> </ol> <h2 id="h_01H6R788W7Z5DJAGKSZSHWXH3X">Schedule repost on a web browser</h2> <p>To schedule reposts on a web browser, you will need to have the <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/653-buffer-browser-extension" target="_self">Buffer browser extension installed</a>.</p> <ol> <li>Find a post you'd like to repost and click the Buffer icon.</li> </ol> <img alt="Buffer icon on a published tweet" src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/652002c842d97d1c04591662/file-rkGI9F5DYZ.png" style="width: 465.99px; max-width: 100%; " /><br /> <ol start="2"> <li>The composer will open on the page, allowing you to add a comment (optional) and then add the post to your queue. </li><a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/642-scheduling-posts#01H7Z9VSXB3C6E1KQ9Y4G6QFY0" target="_self">Guidance on scheduling options can be found here</a>.</li> </ol> <img alt="Buffer retweet in the composer" src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b656a76eb51e63b8f9e7db/file-IjTptgTByy.jpg" style="width: 392.969px; max-width: 100%; " /> <ol start="3"> <li>Another quick option to schedule a repost on the web browser is to copy and paste the original post's URL directly into the Buffer composer (as long as the link is unshortened). If needed, add any commentary ahead of the copied/pasted URL and it will show as a quoted repost.</li> </ol> <div class="callout-blue"> <h4 id="01H800XJ9BXCC13QKHJB8QP6VX">üìù Troubleshooting</h4> <p><b>For Safari users: </b>At this time, it is not possible to schedule X/Twitter reposts in Safari, as we are blocked by some of the requirements for the extension. Apologies for any inconvenience!</p> <p><b>For other browsers: </b>If you have the Buffer Browser Extension installed and do not see the Buffer icon on posts when you are on X/Twitter, please try the following:</p> <ul> <li>Remove the Buffer browser extension from your browser and reinstall it from </li><a href="https://www.buffer.com/extensions/" target="_self" rel="undefined">https://www.buffer.com/extensions/</a>.</li> <li>Check your other browser extensions and VPN, and safelist Buffer where possible. Learn more about </li><a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/508-opening-buffer-in-an-incognito-or-private-window-how-to-safelist-buffer-and-using-a-vpn" target="_self">Opening Buffer in an incognito or private window, how to safelist Buffer, and using a VPN here</a>.</li> <li>Contact our support team at </li><a href="mailto:hello@buffer.com">hello@buffer.com</a> if you still need help.</li> </ul> </div> <h2 id="h_01H6R8JDWZNJS07A2MS2P6XCDR">Schedule reposts on the mobile app</h2> <p>To schedule reposts on the mobile app, you will need to have the <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/603-getting-started-with-buffers-mobile-app" target="_self">Buffer mobile app</a> installed.</p> <ol> <li>Open the X/Twitter app and find a post you'd like to repost. Tap the share icon at the bottom of the post and then tap </li><em><strong>Share via</strong></em>.</li> <li>Tap the </li><em><strong>Buffer</strong></em> icon.</li> <li>The Buffer app will open, with the repostt already loaded into the composer. Feel free to add a comment to the repost if you'd like to.</li> <li>Make sure you have your X/Twitter account selected to share the post to, by tapping the </li><em><strong>+</strong></em> icon at the top of the composer and then tapping your X/Twitter account. You'll then need to tap <em><strong>Done</strong></em> or the back arrow at the top of your screen.</li> <li>Once you're happy with your repost, tap </li><strong><em>Share</em></strong>. <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/642-scheduling-posts" target="_self">Guidance on scheduling options can be found here</a>.</li> </ol> <img alt="Retweet in the Buffer mobile app" src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651499108820396325b6fd95/img-1714411-1695848718-177972413.gif" style="width: 296.988px; max-width: 100%; " /> </article>
