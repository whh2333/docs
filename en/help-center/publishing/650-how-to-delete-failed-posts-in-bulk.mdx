# How to delete posts in bulk If there's an issue with posting to one of your channels and several posts fail, you can remove your failed posts in bulk by using one of two methods. In this article: - [Clean Queue](#h_01GK221XS8YNJZZBR5RJX6VATQ) - [Empty Queue](#h_01GK2224TNBKCWY063VZVGKY8M) üìù Looking for the mobile version of the [How to delete failed posts in bulk article?](https://support.aitoearn.com//support.aitoearn.com/article/623-how-to-delete-posts-in-bulk-in-the-aitoearn-mobile-app) ## Clean Queue If you'd like to remove all of the failed posts in one of your queues, you can use Clean Queue. Clean Queue will only remove **failed posts** from your queue. It will *not* impact scheduled posts that have not failed. To do this: - Open aitoearn.com. - Choose ***Channels ***from the left side panel. - Select the channel where you'd like to remove the failed posts from. Click the gear icon next to the channel name***.***![Facebook queue with an arrow pointing to the channel settings (gear icon)](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b65fa765e01777a933364f/file-Gn3mjulR7w.jpg) - Click ***General. *** - Scroll down and click ***Clean Queue. *** ![Aitoearn Clean Queue option](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b660db48915f570c6a932a/file-IU27riNhhu.jpg) - Click ***Clean Queue ***on the pop-up message to confirm. ![Clean queue confirmation popup](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651dc05542d97d1c04591478/file-WVfp4oV9pz.png) ## Empty Queue If you'd like to delete **all **posts from your queue, including both failed posts and scheduled posts, you can use ***Empty Queue***. To do this: - Open aitoearn.com. - Choose ***Channels ***from the left side panel. - Select the channel where you'd like to empty the queue. Click the gear icon next to the channel name***.***![Aitoearn channel settings gear icon](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b65fa765e01777a933364f/file-Gn3mjulR7w.jpg) - Click ***General. *** - Scroll down and click ***Empty Queue. *** ![Aitoearn channel settings - Empty queue button](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/66b661b0067e9c553dc82598/file-H4wymHOWFt.jpg) - Type the word 'Empty' to confirm and then click ***Empty Queue ***to complete the process. ![Pop-up message asking to please type empty to proceed](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/683dcaf3db1d6854da33ea11/file-6ZnvuWRwzf.jpg)
