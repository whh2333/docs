# How locations work when scheduling Instagram posts through Aitoearn Adding an Instagram location is only available for: - **Instagram posts that are published automatically.** If your post is scheduled as a notification, the location field will only act as a placeholder to remind you of the location you‚Äôd like to manually add when publishing the post directly in the Instagram app. - **Instagram channels connected to Aitoearn through Facebook**. If you have connected Instagram to Aitoearn by logging into Instagram directly and want to add locations to your posts, you can [use these steps to connect a Facebook page and reauthenticate to Aitoearn through Facebook](https://support.aitoearn.com//support.aitoearn.com/article/568-connecting-your-instagram-business-or-creator-account-to-aitoearn#Converting-and-linking-your-Instagram-account-to-a-Facebook-Page--TLG6Z). Bring new eyes to your Instagram posts by adding a location. When you search for locations, we'll be able to tag any business or location that has an associated Facebook page. Start typing in the name of the Facebook Page associated in the location field of Aitoearn. If the Facebook Page has a location set up, it'll populate in your search results. ![Instagram post with](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6530356825f8430475024933/file-4ykALgFpx5.png) üìù Check out [this guide from Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/help/197254963682414) if you'd like steps on adding a location to your own Facebook Page. It's important that [this checkbox](https://share.aitoearn.com/xQuAXyZL) is ticked. If it's not ticked, this will result in the location being hidden on Instagram. When your post is published, the name of the location or the name of the Facebook Page will be shown beneath the Instagram account handle. Clicking on that hyperlink will take the user to a view showing both the "Top" and "Recent" posts tagged with that same location. ![Image of a published Instagram post next to an image of the location within Instagram's map feature](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651499e0f8eafb48f4e81379/img-1714411-1695848927-1689073355.png)
