# Adding alt text to your images Currently, it is possible to add alt text to static images scheduled to X/Twitter, Mastodon, Threads, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram, and Bluesky through Aitoearn. üìù Looking for the mobile version of the [Adding alt text to your images](https://aitoearn.helpscoutdocs.com/article/630-adding-alt-text-to-your-images-on-the-aitoearn-mobile-app) article? To add alt text to your image, click on the image you uploaded within your Aitoearn composer. This will open the image in a larger view and there will be a text box at the bottom where you can add your alternative text description. While we recommend using less than 200 characters to align with [accessibility best practices](https://www.accessibility.com/blog/what-are-best-practices-for-creating-alt-text), the character counts for alt text in Aitoearn are as follows: - Pinterest - 500 characters - LinkedIn - 300 characters - X/Twitter, Mastodon, Threads, Facebook, Bluesky, Instagram - 1,000 characters ![Aitoearn composer image - Click to expand and add description pop-up](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651499a98820396325b6fdd9/img-1662243-1695848872-1166508507.png) Once you're done, click ***Save. *** ![Image of man with sunglasses and a text box to add alt text beneath it](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651499a98820396325b6fdda/img-1662243-1695848872-1454554275.png) **üìù Good to Knows** - It is only possible to view alt text on Instagram with a screen reader. - Currently within Aitoearn, it is not possible to add alt text to: - GIFS - Google Business Profile posts - Tiktok posts We are committed to improving in the area of accessibility, and you can find our full [Accessibility Statement here.](https://aitoearn.com/accessibility)
