<h2 id="h_77d80241-2003-43bf-93a3-75293b9e109a">Finding your report</h2>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>üìù Audience data is available only for Instagram (not Facebook, X/Twitter, or LinkedIn).</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>Start by clicking <i><b>Analyze</b></i> at the top of your Buffer dashboard (not available on mobile).</li>
 <li>Select the channel you'd like to analyze on the left-hand side of your dashboard.</li>
 <li>Choose a profile or Page from the drop-down.</li>
 <li>Click&nbsp;<strong><em>Audience</em></strong>.</li>
 <li>Choose the date range you'd like to view.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="h_12a9d0fb-4611-49de-8139-8feb5b8e663a">Diving into your report</h2>
<h3 id="h_01HCXNKFVPX7Q4C5JBPY05XPQ5">Audience overview</h3>
<p>Curious about which demographics are engaging with your content? The audience overview chart shows your top demographic by gender and age, as well as your top location.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6658922add0f8c60bb3fc749/file-t5ejcn3hf1.jpg" alt="Buffer Analyze Audience overview" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h3 id="h_01HCXNKFVP8GR9E41KCTY0RHXJ">Top gender and age</h3>
<p>The top gender and age chart displays a bubble chart of females and males, by age. Below the bubble chart, you'll see your top three ranked demographics.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6514995f556a3e3cf18cbffb/img-1656503-1695848797-469231751.png" alt="Top gender and age bubble chart in audience analytics" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h3 id="h_01HCXNKFVPXDCTQJ4VMEGFDP68">Top cities</h3>
<p>The top cities chart displays the top five cities where your fans are located. Below the chart, you'll see your top three ranked cities.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6514995f5416dd630a4b0691/img-1656503-1695848798-1593102282.png" alt="Bar chart showing top five cities where your fans are located" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h3 id="h_01HCXNKFVQQTE74KY78VYAH5VE">Top countries</h3>
<p>The top countries chart displays a map of where your fans are located. You can hover over areas on the map to see the number and percentage of fans. Below the map, you'll see your top three ranked countries.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/651499606241295a81ba6db4/img-1656503-1695848798-625250390.png" alt="World map displaying where your fans are located" style="width: 100%; "></p>
<h2 id="h_db8f6272-8e7f-4b52-932a-64075f6156f9">Adding charts to a custom report</h2>
<p>Finally, you can add any of the tables and charts from this area to a custom report. Simply click the plus button at the top right of any chart you'd like to include. <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/534-creating-custom-analytics-reports" target="_self"><strong>Learn more about creating custom reports here</strong></a>.</p>
<p>You can also export all data as both CSV and image files using the <em><strong>Export as...</strong></em> button.</p>
