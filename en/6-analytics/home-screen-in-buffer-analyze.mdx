<h2 id="h_01ERJ8M0T20NYSA3B4FJ6CFMXM">Finding your report</h2>
<p>On your analytics <i><b>Home</b></i> page, you'll find combined metrics across all channels for the last seven days (there isn't a way to add a custom date range). You'll access it by clicking on the "Analyze" tab at the top of the web dashboard (not available on mobile).</p>

<h2 id="h_01ERJ8M0T20NYSA3B4FJ6CFMXM">Totals</h2>

<h2 id="h_01ERJ8MEBYJZ6R1DK8G5PSXA6H">Recent posts</h2>
<p>This section shows your most recent posts across your connected social channels and the reach* and engagement rate for each post. Click on any post to find more detailed information in the Posts tab&nbsp;for that social channel. Learn more about <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/527-analyzing-your-posts-within-your-analytics-dashboard">Analyzing posts within your analytics dashboard</a>.</p>
<p><i>*Reach is no longer available for Facebook Pages, due to changes from Meta</i></p>
