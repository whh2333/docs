        <h1 class="title">How to avoid being logged out when using the Buffer browser extension in Safari</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>If you're using Safari and find that you're frequently logged out of Buffer's <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/653-buffer-browser-extension" target="_self">browser extension</a>, changing your privacy settings can help. Please follow the steps below.</p>
<ol>
 <li>In Safari, click <em><strong>Safari</strong></em> and then <em><strong>Settings</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Select the <em><strong>Privacy</strong></em> tab and then un-check the "Prevent cross-site tracking" box.</li>
 <li>Restart Safari and you should be all set!</li>
</ol>
