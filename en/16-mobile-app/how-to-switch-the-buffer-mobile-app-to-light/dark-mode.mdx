        <h1 class="title">How to switch the Buffer mobile app to light/dark mode</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>In order to switch the display of your Buffer mobile app, you'll need to adjust the display settings within your phone's settings.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Here's how to do this:&nbsp;</p>
<h2 id="h_01HC04B8P0ZAQ85A1PY70TKAVV">iOS</h2>
<ol>
 <li>On your phone, open the <em><strong>Settings&nbsp;</strong></em>app.&nbsp;</li>
 <li>Select&nbsp;<em><strong>Display &amp; Brightness.&nbsp;</strong></em></li>
 <li>Under "Appearance" select the mode you prefer to use (<em><strong>Light</strong></em> or <em><strong>Dark</strong></em>). This should change everything to light or dark, including the Buffer app.&nbsp;</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="h_01HC04B8P0866APDWHWNT8G7ES">Android</h2>
<ol>
 <li>On your phone, open the <em><strong>Settings</strong></em> app.</li>
 <li>Tap <em><strong>Display</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Turn <em><strong>Dark theme</strong></em> on or off.&nbsp;This should change everything to light mode or dark mode, including the Buffer app.&nbsp;</li>
</ol><br>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>Dark mode is not available for the desktop version of Buffer at this time. If you'd like to see us support this in the future, <a href="https://suggestions.buffer.com/p/dark-mode">you can upvote this on our feature suggestions board</a>.</p>
</div>
