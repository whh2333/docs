<h2 id="Accessing-the-All-Channels-view-g5OhJ">Accessing the All Channels view</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Tap on the menu icon in the upper left of your screen.</li>
 <li>Under "Channels" tap <i><b>All Channels.</b></i></li>
</ol>
<p>This will display your content across all channels. Read on to learn how you can filter this content by content type, channel, and tag.</p>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6792bbe366d1ed4003f76094/file-JuZgT9dTCe.png" alt="All channels option in the Buffer mobile app" style="width: 296.982px; "></p>
<h2 id="Filtering-by-content-type-0Rm3w">Filtering by content type</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Tap on the menu icon in the upper left of your screen.</li>
 <li>Under "Channels" tap <i><b>All Channels.</b></i></li>
 <li>Next, tap one of the tabs at the top of the screen to filter your content:</li>
</ol>
<ul>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Queue </b></i>to view scheduled content.</li>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Drafts </b></i>to view a list of draft posts.</li>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Approvals </b></i>to view posts waiting to be approved.</li>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Sent </b></i>to view your published posts.</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6792bb2066d1ed4003f7608e/file-xEAAJNSATV.png" alt="Buffer mobile Queue, Drafts, Approvals, and Sent options" style="width: 319.98px; "></p>
<h2 id="Filtering-by-channel-jZaWF">Filtering by channel</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Tap on the menu icon in the upper left of your screen.</li>
 <li>Under "Channels" tap <i><b>All Channels.</b></i></li>
 <li>Tap the channel(s) you'd like to filter by and then tap <i><b>Close. </b></i></li>
</ol>
<p><img src="//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/64cb6cf86d4a3e191f237e66/images/6792b9f782f22a4614b8da5f/file-SfqWcK8tbv.png" alt="Buffer mobile app All Channels view - filter by channel" style="width: 322.979px; "></p>
<h2 id="Filtering-by-tag-druij">Filtering by tag</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Tap on the menu icon in the upper left of your screen.</li>
 <li>Under "Channels" tap <i><b>All Channels.</b></i></li>
 <li>Tap the tag(s) you'd like to filter by and then tap <i><b>Close. </b></i></li>
</ol>
