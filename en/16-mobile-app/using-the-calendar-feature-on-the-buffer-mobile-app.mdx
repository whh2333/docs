<h2 id="h_01FGT3JH0381YE085MR0KKAP2E">Navigating to the calendar view on mobile</h2>
<p>Open the Buffer app on your mobile device, and tap on the three horizontal lines in the upper left-hand menu, then tap <em><strong>Calendar. </strong></em>From the Calendar, you can see scheduled posts in a monthly view, or access a daily view by tapping on a date in the calendar (ex. September 30th). On Android, click the blue pencil icon in the lower left-hand corner. At this time, we don't support a weekly view in the mobile version of the calendar, but there is a daily view.</p>

<h2 id="h_01FGT6FMTF3ZR7YADECW49KW99">Editing Posts in the calendar</h2>
<p>Edit posts by tapping on the date in the calendar, then tapping on the ellipsis ("...") located in the top right corner of the post. Tap <em><strong>Edit this post</strong></em> to make changes.</p>
