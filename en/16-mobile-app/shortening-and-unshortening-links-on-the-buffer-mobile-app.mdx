        <h1 class="title">Shortening and unshortening links on the Buffer mobile app</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>Shortening links is a common practice when sharing URLs on social media so they have a smaller presence or character count than a full URL. By default, links in Buffer will not be shortened, however, you can choose to have your links shortened automatically if you prefer.</p>
<p>In this article:</p>
<ol>
 <li><a href="#Changing-your-link-shortener-poR8w" target="_self">Enabling link shortening</a></li>
 <li><a href="#Unshortening-links-vwNxQ" target="_self">Unshortening links by default </a></li>
 <li><a href="#Setting-up-your-custom-link-shortener-RgaMh">Setting up your custom link shortener</a></li>
</ol>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <ul>
  <li>As of April 17, 2025, all channels for Free users will be set to "No Shortening" by default, including those that had&nbsp;<a href="http://buff.ly/">buff.ly</a>&nbsp;enabled previously. However, you can enable link shortening for your channels using the steps below.</li>
  <li>Looking for the web version of <a href="https://support.buffer.com//support.buffer.com/article/620-shortening-and-unshortening-links">Shortening and unshortening links</a>?</li>
 </ul>
</div>
<h2 id="Changing-your-link-shortener-poR8w">Enabling link shortening</h2>
<p>On the mobile app, tap the three horizontal lines in the upper left corner and select the channel where you'd like to enable link shortening, then follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
 <li>In the bottom right corner of the app, tap&nbsp;<i><b>Settings</b></i></li>
 <li>Make sure the channel settings are selected at the top of the app - you should see the name of the channel listed, like "[Channel name] Settings"</li>
 <li>Under the "Composing" area, tap&nbsp;<i><b>Link Shortening&nbsp;</b></i>and select&nbsp;buff.ly.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="Unshortening-links-vwNxQ">Unshortening links by default</h2>
<p>First, tap the three horizontal lines in the upper left corner of the app and select the channel where you'd like to turn off link shortening. Then:</p>
<ol>
 <li>In the bottom right corner of the app, tap&nbsp;<i><b>Settings</b></i></li>
 <li>Make sure the channel settings are selected at the top of the app - you should see the name of the channel listed.</li>
 <li>Under the "Composing" area, tap&nbsp;<i><b>Link Shortening&nbsp;</b></i>and select&nbsp;<i><b>No Shortening&nbsp;</b></i>to turn link shortening off for that channel.</li>
</ol>
