<article id="fullArticle">
        <h1 class="title">Setting up your timezones and posting schedules on the Buffer mobile app</h1>
    
        <a href="javascript:window.print()" class="printArticle" title="Print this article" aria-label="Print this Article"><i class="icon-print"></i></a>
        <p>We sometimes want to be very specific about the date and time we schedule a post to publish, but we shouldn’t have to think about it every time. Setting up a posting schedule for each of your social channels will free you from making the same decision over and over.</p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>📝 Looking for the web version of the <a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/514-setting-up-your-timezones-and-posting-schedules" target="_self">Setting up your timezones and posting schedule article</a>?&nbsp;</p>
</div>
<h2 id="01H2XHRXF2K74GWKV45NY4QP1A">Setting up your timezone and posting schedule</h2>
<p>When you add a time to a channel’s posting schedule, it becomes a time slot in the channel’s queue. Then, when you use the “Add to Queue” option to schedule a post, the post will automatically be placed into the next available time slot and will publish at that corresponding date and time.</p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>📝 <b>Note: </b>Each social channel has its own timezone settings. Follow these steps for each of your channels to ensure your posts publish when intended.</p>
</div>
<ol>
 <li>Tap the menu icon at the top left of your screen.</li>
 <li>Tap on the channel you'd like to adjust the timezone or posting schedule for.</li>
 <li>Tap the Settings icon at the bottom right of your screen.</li>
 <li>Tap <i><b>Posting Schedule </b></i>to change your timezone or to add and remove posting times.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="01H2XHW4P8BCMDGAJA2KC3VQ3V">A few more details:</h3>
<ul>
 <li>To edit or delete an existing posting time, select it and tap Remove or change the time and tap <em><strong>Use this time</strong></em>.</li>
 <li>Hide posting times in your queue for any day by clicking into that day and toggling off the setting, “Enable [day name] Posting Times.”</li>
</ul>
