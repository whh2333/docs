<h2>Scheduling Posts</h2>
<ol>
 <li>Tap the + button at the bottom of your screen. This will open up the composer.&nbsp;</li>
 <li>The social channel you were viewing before opening the composer will already be selected. If you'd like to share to multiple channels, tap the + icon and then tap any channels you'd like to include. Finally, tap <em><strong>Done</strong></em> or the back arrow at the top of your screen.&nbsp;</li>
</ol>
