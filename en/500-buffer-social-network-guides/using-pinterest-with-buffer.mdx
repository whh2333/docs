<h2 id="01H8ETQEKAK7NYXD98XW6DXR8R">Connecting and refreshing your Pinterest channel</h2>
<p>Head to your Channels area to connect your Pinterest channel: <a href="https://account.buffer.com/channels">https://account.buffer.com/channels</a></p>
<p>You can connect multiple Pinterest accounts to Buffer. To make sure Buffer knows which one want to connect, log in to that Pinterest account in another browser tab before trying to connect it to Buffer.</p>
<p>Lastly, if your connected Pinterest channel requires a refresh, check out the article&nbsp;<a href="https://buffer.helpscoutdocs.com/article/573-refreshing-a-channel-in-buffer">Refreshing a channel in Buffer</a>.</p>
<div class="callout-blue">
 <p>If you have <a href="https://help.pinterest.com/en/article/switch-between-accounts">a Pinterest business account linked to your Pinterest personal account</a>, you'll need to first log into Pinterest and make sure the account you want to connect to Buffer is currently selected from the dropdown in the upper right corner of Pinterest. Once the correct account is selected, you can head back to Buffer to connect the Pinterest account.</p>

<h2 id="How-to-add-a-thumbnail-to-your-video-pOeNi">How to add a thumbnail to your video</h2>
<p>By default, the first frame of the video (0 seconds) will be used as the thumbnail. If you'd like to change your thumbnail, you'll follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
 <li>Once your video has finished uploading, click&nbsp;<em><strong>Edit Thumbnail</strong></em><em>,</em></li>
 <li>Drag the slider to the position in the video you'd like to use as the cover/thumbnail image.</li>
 <li>Click&nbsp;<em><strong>Save</strong></em>.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="01H8EYAASWEN45S4H3AJ1522QW">Engaging with Pinterest posts</h2>
<p>Buffer's engagement features are only available for Facebook and Instagram. Pinterest is not supported within Buffer's engagement features at this time.</p>
<h2 id="01H8EYAASWF6DNS3CH3624CFJD">Pinterest analytics</h2>
<p>You can view basic analytics for each of your Pinterest posts' metrics by heading to the&nbsp;<em><strong>Publish</strong></em>&nbsp;tab and then the&nbsp;<em><strong>Sent</strong></em>&nbsp;tab.</p>
